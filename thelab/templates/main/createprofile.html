About Lab Profiles

Levels:

  • Athlete: Every user (athlete, parent, or coach) starts as an "athlete". Athletes can access content (packages, events, drills).
  • Coach: Athletes can upgrade their profile to coach by submitting an application that verifies college-minimum playing or coaching experience. Coaches can create content (packages, events, drills).

Core Capabilities:

  • Search and register for events with ease (for yourself or your kids).
  • View upcoming events and track registration history.
  • Save and complete expertly-crafted drills (coming soon)-- by recording and submitting your drill performance to a coach, you can receive expert feedback (and score confirmation, which is required to participate in Drill Leaderboards).

Exclusive Coach Tools:

  • Host and monetize events effortlessly.
  • Manage business accounts with integrated scheduling.
  • Post interactive drills for remote training (coming soon).

** Event registration does not require a profile. Attendees must be named but can differ from the registering user. All registrants must be 18 or older.
