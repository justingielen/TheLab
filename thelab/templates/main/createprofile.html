About Lab Profiles

Levels:

  • Basic: Every user (athlete, parent, or coach) starts as a basic user. Users can access content (packages, events, drills).
  • Coach: Users can upgrade their profile to coach by submitting an application that verifies college-minimum playing or coaching experience. Coaches can create content (packages, events, drills).

Core Capabilities:

  • Find qualified coaches and purchase coaching services with ease.
  • Seamlessly search and register for events and track registration history.
  • (coming soon) Discover and complete expertly-crafted drills in your remote workouts-- record and submit your drill performance to a Lab coach and you can receive expert, personalized feedback (and score confirmation, to participate in drill leaderboards and community competitions).

Exclusive Coach Tools:

  • Monetize coaching service packages (e.g., $50/hr private training) from your page.
  • Create and host coaching events effortlessly.
  • Manage business accounts with integrated scheduling (coming soon).
  • Post interactive drills for remote training (coming soon).

** Event registration does not require a profile. Attendees simply must be named. All registrants must be 18 or older.
